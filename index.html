Let's Find Us

List of Major

Electrical Engineering

The Department of Industrial Electronics Engineering is a branch of engineering that focuses on the development, design and maintenance of electronic systems used in industrial contexts.

Computer Network Engineering

The Computer Engineering Department is a vocational education program in the field of information technology and computer networks. Students in this major will gain the knowledge and skills needed to design, manage, and maintain computer networks.

Machining Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering Department is a vocational program that emphasizes the mastery of technical skills and knowledge in the field of machinery and manufacturing. Students in this department are trained to become competent machining technicians who are ready to enter the industrial world.

Light Vehicle Engineering

The Light Vehicle Engineering Department is a vocational program that focuses on technical skills and knowledge related to repair, maintenance and diagnosis of four-wheeled motorized vehicles. Students in this major are trained to become skilled light vehicle technicians and have a deep understanding of vehicle engines.
